Welcome to SMARTS
=================

SMARTS (Scalable Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning Training School) is a simulation platform for multi-agent research on autonomous driving. Its focus is on realistic and diverse interactions.

Check out the paper at `SMARTS: Scalable Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning Training School for Autonomous Driving <https://arxiv.org/abs/2010.09776>`_ .
