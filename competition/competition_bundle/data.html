Track-1

(a) There are 8 scenarios provided for training, namely,
  • 1_to_2lane_left_turn_c
  • 1_to_2lane_left_turn_t
  • 3lane_merge_multi_agent
  • 3lane_merge_single_agent
  • 3lane_cruise_multi_agent
  • 3lane_cruise_single_agent
  • 3lane_cut_in
  • 3lane_overtake
(b) The scenarios are pre-built when instantiating the environment.

env = gym.make(
    "smarts.env:multi-scenario-v0",
    scenario=<name-of-scenario>
)
(c) Participants may choose to alter the pre-built scenarios by modifying the corresponding scenario.py and map.net.xml files, in order to create more scenarios and maps for higher training diversity.
(d) Refer to Scenario Studio to build and modify new scenarios.
